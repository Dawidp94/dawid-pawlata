about me

My name is Dawid Pawlata, I'm 28 years old and I come from a small town in Lodz province - Lowicz. I discovered my passion for programming back in my high school days, when we created our first websites during computer science class. Creating templates, styling images or creating animations, when all this seemed to me totally incomprehensible and alien. However, the satisfaction of the first correctly written lines of code, the first :hover effects, the first working hyperlinks, or the first smoothly working animations, completely compensates for the many hours of effort in search of a solution.

High school times passed, but the passion and curiosity for programming remained. A year ago I made the decision that this would be my career path. Over the past year I have been persistently learning new programming languages, learning new frameworks and finding interesting libraries. I completed a javascript front-end developer course, which helped me understand front-end and back-end techniques and issues. I got to know the node.js environment, which allowed me to discover more possibilities, including the use of Tailwind css framework and React libraries.
